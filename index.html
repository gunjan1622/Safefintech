About NFLQ

The National Finance Literacy Quiz is India's premier platform for students to demonstrate their financial knowledge and win recognition.

About NFLQ 2025

NFLQ 2025 is a national-level competition organized by the Sodhian Academy of Fintech Enablers Limited. Its mission is to educate and empower students with essential financial skills, helping them navigate the world of money management, cyber frauds, scams, and financial planning. The competition is designed to be both educational and enjoyable, offering students a practical and engaging experience with real-life money concepts.

Eligibility Categories

The competition is open to students across different age groups and educational levels.

Junior Category

Classes 6-8

For students beginning their journey in financial literacy with age-appropriate concepts.

  • Basic banking concepts
  • Introduction to saving
  • Money management basics

Intermediate Category

Classes 9-10

For students ready to explore more complex financial concepts and applications.

  • Banking products and services
  • Introduction to investments
  • Financial planning basics

Senior Category

Classes 11-12

For students preparing for higher education with advanced financial knowledge.

  • Investment strategies
  • Personal finance management
  • Introduction to markets

College Category

Undergraduate Students

For college students seeking comprehensive financial literacy and expertise.

  • Advanced investment analysis
  • Taxation and financial planning
  • Market dynamics and economics
